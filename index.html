Our Story

Nestled in the bustling heart of Maharagama, Ceylon Brew Café is your daily escape to the cool, misty hills of the island. While our doors open to the city's rhythm, our soul is rooted in the lush plantations of Nuwara Eliya, where we source our premium Arabica beans. We believe in the magic of a perfect brew—one that carries the whisper of the hill country breeze and the warmth of Sri Lankan hospitality. Whether you're catching up on work or sharing a laugh with friends, we invite you to savor a cup that bridges the gap between the vibrant city life and the serene tranquility of the mountains.
